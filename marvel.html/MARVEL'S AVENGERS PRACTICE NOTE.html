  1. IRON-MAN

    Iron Man (Tony Stark): A billionaire genius and philanthropist who dons an advanced suit of armor. Tony Stark is the founding member of the Avengers, using his wealth and intellect to create cutting-edge technology and weapons. He is known for his quick wit, leadership skills, and his journey from a self-centered industrialist to a self-sacrificing hero.

  2. THOR

    Thor: Norse God of Thunder, Thor wields the mighty hammer Mjolnir and possesses superhuman strength, the ability to control lightning, and a strong sense of justice. Thor is known for his noble and sometimes arrogant personality, but he grows to be a wise and compassionate leader.

  3. CAPTAIN AMERICA

    Captain America (Steve Rogers): Enhanced to the peak of human physical potential by an experimental serum, Steve Rogers is a patriotic super-soldier known for his unwavering morality and indestructible vibranium shield. He is the moral compass of the Avengers and a symbol of hope and perseverance.

  4. HULK

    Hulk (Bruce Banner): A brilliant scientist who transforms into a massive green-skinned behemoth when angered, the Hulk possesses near-limitless strength and durability. Bruce Banner constantly struggles with controlling his alter ego, striving to use the Hulk's power for good.

  5. BLACK WIDOW

    Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff): A highly skilled spy and assassin with expertise in hand-to-hand combat and espionage. Natasha Romanoff brings a sharp intellect and deadly skill set to the team. She has a mysterious past and is constantly seeking redemption for her earlier life as a double agent.

  6. BLACK PANTHER

    Black Panther (T'Challa): T'Challa is the king of Wakanda and inherits the title of Black Panther. Enhanced by a mystical herb, he gains superhuman abilities. As a leader and warrior, he uses advanced Wakandan technology to protect his people and fight for justice globally. T'Challa is also known for his wisdom, diplomacy, and commitment to his people.

  7. DOCTOR STRANGE

    Doctor Strange (Stephen Strange): A former neurosurgeon turned Master of the Mystic Arts, Stephen Strange wields powerful sorcery and defends the Earth against mystical threats. His journey from a gifted but arrogant surgeon to a selfless sorcerer demonstrates his growth and dedication to protecting reality.

  8. HAWKEYE

    Hawkeye (Clint Barton): A master archer and marksman, Clint Barton uses his unparalleled archery skills and various trick arrows to fight alongside the Avengers. Despite lacking superhuman abilities, his precision, bravery, and loyalty make him a vital member of the team.

  9. SCARLET WITCH

    Scarlet Witch (Wanda Maximoff): With powers of telekinesis, energy manipulation, and reality-warping, Wanda Maximoff is one of the most powerful members of the Avengers. Her abilities stem from a combination of experimentation and innate potential, making her a formidable force.

  10. SPIDER-MAN

    Spider-Man (Peter Parker): A young superhero with spider-like abilities, including wall-crawling, enhanced strength, agility, and a "spidey sense" that warns him of danger. He uses web-shooters of his own invention. Peter balances his superhero duties with the challenges of everyday life, embodying the spirit of responsibility and perseverance.

  11. VISION

    Vision: An android created by Ultron but reprogrammed to be an ally, Vision possesses the Mind Stone, granting him a variety of powers including density manipulation, flight, and energy projection. Vision represents the fusion of humanity and technology, constantly seeking to understand human emotions and ethics.
